# My AI & Machine Learning Journey 🚀

Welcome to my AI and Machine Learning repository! This repository serves as a personal log of my progress, containing the code, notes, and projects I'm building to master Data Science, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and NLP. 

## 🎯 My Learning Path

My journey is split into two phases: building a strong foundation, and then diving deep into the mathematics and from-scratch implementations.

### ✅ Phase 1: The Foundation (Completed)
I have successfully completed the **Complete A.I. & Machine Learning, Data Science Bootcamp** (by ZTM / Andrei Neagoie & Daniel Bourke). 
This gave me a robust, hands-on foundation in the entire data science workflow, including:
*   **Data Science Frameworks:** Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib.
*   **Machine Learning Models:** Scikit-Learn workflows, Classification, Regression, Model Evaluation (Cross-validation, ROC, Confusion Matrix), and Hyperparameter Tuning.
*   **Deep Learning & Data Engineering:** TensorFlow 2, Google Colab, Transfer Learning, and an introduction to Data Engineering (Hadoop, Spark, Kafka).
*   **Projects:** End-to-end milestone projects involving structured and unstructured data (image classification, bulldozers price prediction, heart disease detection).

### 🚀 Phase 2: In-Depth Mastery (Current Focus)
I am currently revisiting the core concepts *in much greater depth* through the **Complete Data Science, Machine Learning, DL, NLP Bootcamp** (by Krish Naik). My goal here is to deeply understand the underlying mathematics, statistical assumptions, and practical implementations of every algorithm. 

**Progress in Phase 2 (Completed up to Support Vector Machines):**
*   **Python & Advanced Concepts:** Deep dive into OOP, Iterators, Generators, Decorators, Memory Allocation, Exception Handling, and Logging.
*   **Web Frameworks & Parallelism:** Building Streamlit and Flask apps; working with Multithreading and Multiprocessing.
*   **Statistics & Probability:** Comprehensive study of Descriptive Statistics, Probability Distributions (Normal, Binomial, Log-Normal, Pareto), Central Limit Theorem, Hypothesis Testing (Z-Test, T-Test, ANOVA, Chi-Square), and Bayes' Theorem.
*   **Feature Engineering:** Advanced handling of imbalanced datasets (SMOTE), outliers, and data encoding.
*   **Machine Learning (Math & Implementation):**
    *   **Linear Regression:** Simple/Multiple/Polynomial Regression, OLS, Cost Functions, Convergence Algorithms.
    *   **Regularization:** Ridge, Lasso, and Elastic Net with Cross-Validation.
    *   **Logistic Regression:** OVR, ROC, Mathematical Intuition.
    *   **Support Vector Machines (SVM):** Soft/Hard Margins, SVC, SVR, and Kernel mathematical intuition.
